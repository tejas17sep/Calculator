⦁	Charge entry and Claim Submission
Charge entry: Capturing Charge, coding, and Provider information in the billing software with the help of Charge sheet/Charge ticket/Super Bill.
Claim submission: Creating and submitting the claim to payer.
Types of Claim Submission:
⦁	E-CLAIM Sent through electronic transmission of data (Clearing house)
Clearing house Checks all the eClaims if they have any errors, eClaims with errors will be rejected for correction, eCaims without errors will be converted to insurance specific format & error free e-claims will be sent to insurance company.
⦁	P-CLAIM Sent through postal services and fax (When requested by Payer)

⦁	Claim Adjudication: Claims Adjudication or Claims examination is the process of analyzing the claim and decide whether to pay a claim, deny a claim and pend/hold a claim. Post adjudication provider will get the payment and Payment EOB/Denial EOB as a communication of Decision.

Receivable Management:

⦁	Payment Posting: Capturing payment and denial information in the software with the help of payment EOB/Denial EOB/Correspondence and Cheque Copy.

Mode of payment: EFT Electronic Fund Transfer and Cheque.

⦁	Account Receivable: Account receivable is a team which follows up with insurance for pending balance and will work according to get the payment from payer. Also responsible for denial management.
Ways to check claim status Online (Insurance webportal, chat, mail), IVR (Interactive Voice Response) and Live Representative.

⦁	Patient Support: Patient support is a team which follows up with the patient regarding pending balance from patient such as copay, coinsurance and deductible.
